Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum CIBIL score required?

ABC Bank recommends maintaining a CIBIL score above 700.

How long does loan approval take?

Usually between 24 to 72 hours depending on verification.

What documents are required?

PAN card, Aadhaar card, salary slips and bank statements.

What types of loans does ABC Bank offer?

Personal, Home, Vehicle and Business loans.

Can I track my loan status online?

Yes, through the Approval Status section.
